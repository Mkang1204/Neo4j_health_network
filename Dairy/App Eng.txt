Senior Application Engineer- IT Systems
Job Summary
MathWorks is seeking a highly motivated individual with technical experience and interest in integrating MathWorks products with enterprise IT systems. By deeply engaging and partnering with customers in this area, you will influence the way they develop and verify their systems using MathWorks products. The work will involve recommending technical approaches using the existing products and developing proof of concept software solutions for potential future products. Travel can be expected anywhere from 5% to 30%, generally throughout US to EMEA and Asia.

Responsibilities
Establish instant credibility in front of customers by using your industry experience to integrate MathWorks products with enterprise IT systems
Lead technical projects with key enterprise customers 
Develop software reference architectures and extensions to MathWorks products to meet customer needs
Work closely with internal sales and other customer-facing staff to provide technical guidance for successful adoption of MathWorks products
Provide feedback to Development on MathWorks products based on customer interactions/requirements.
Minimum Qualifications
A bachelor's degree and 7 years of professional work experience (or a master's degree and 5 years of professional work experience, or a PhD degree) is required.
Additional Qualifications
Programming knowledge of one or more of: Java, JavaScript, Python, SQL, C/C++.
Knowledge of MATLAB and Simulink is a plus
Broad interest in IT systems integration. Domains of particular interest are:
Big Data, e.g. Hadoop & Spark.
Cloud, e.g. Azure, AWS & GCP
Messaging and IoT related technologies, e.g. Kafka & IoT Hub.
Databases, e.g. MongoDB, SQL Server & Cassandra
Visualization, e.g. Tableau, Power BI & Spotfire.
Customer-facing experience
Excellent spoken and written English communication skills
